Welcome to the Collegiate Archery Society

We are the Collegiate Archery Society, a group focused on college students who have an interest in archery. We are chapter-based and have many local organizations across dozens of universities. Our clubs hold meetings to discuss the sport, sign up for and organize tournaments (both internal and competitive), explore the history of archery, teach technical skills, and, of course, practice! We are committed to keeping this ancient sport alive and active in a fun and exciting way.
